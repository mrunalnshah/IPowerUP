Introduction to TRADEMARKS

A trademark is a symbol, word, or phrase that is legally registered or established by use as representing a company or product. It helps customers identify and distinguish products or services from one company to another. Trademarks can take the form of a brand name, logo, slogan, or even a sound.

Protecting a trademark ensures that other businesses cannot use the same or a similar mark that could confuse customers. It provides legal protection and serves as an important asset in a companyâ€™s intellectual property portfolio.
